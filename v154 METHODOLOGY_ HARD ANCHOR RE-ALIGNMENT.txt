   * Tier 2 (Progression): The remaining 46 files are assigned to Ranks 5-50, sorted strictly by energy score.

* Anchor Prioritization: By enforcing the position of the known healthy states (derived from the centroid of previous best-scoring submissions), this methodology filters out "false negative" degradation signals (sensor dropouts) that violate the physical timeline.
* File 15 Validation: Incorporates the finding from v152 that file_15 is the true "Genesis" file, grouping it with the historical healthy set to form a robust starting cluster.
Key Innovation:
* Hybrid Supervised/Unsupervised Sorting: Unlike previous fully unsupervised submissions, this approach injects domain knowledge (the identity of the healthy cluster) to constrain the solution space, preventing local energy minima from disrupting the global timeline.
* Topological Constraint: Forces the timeline to originate from the confirmed healthy state, ensuring that the monotonicity of the remaining 46 files is calculated relative to the correct $t=0$ reference point.

3. Progression Sort:
   * Isolate the remaining 46 files.

4. Assembly: Concatenate Anchors + Progression + Incident Files.
